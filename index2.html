Seifree Launches Next-Generation Optical Coupler Series

June 10, 2023 | Product News

We are excited to announce the launch of our new series of high-performance optical couplers, designed to meet the growing demands of modern optical networks. These next-generation devices feature improved signal integrity, lower insertion loss, and enhanced durability in harsh environments.

The new series includes models optimized for various applications, from data center interconnects to long-haul telecommunications. Our engineering team has incorporated advanced materials and innovative designs to achieve industry-leading performance metrics.

Seifree Expands Manufacturing Capacity

April 3, 2023 | Company News

To meet growing global demand for our products, Seifree has completed a major expansion of our Wuhan manufacturing facility. The 10,000 square meter addition includes state-of-the-art clean rooms and automated production lines that will increase our capacity by 40%.

"This expansion represents our commitment to delivering high-quality optical components with shorter lead times," said Sarah Zhang, CEO of Seifree. "The new facility incorporates the latest manufacturing technologies while maintaining our rigorous quality standards."
